Frequently Asked Questions

How does the object detection feature work?

Our object detection feature uses advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to identify objects in real-time through your device's camera. The system recognizes objects, determines their position relative to the user, and provides audio feedback about what's being detected.

Is my data secure when using Blind Vision?

Yes, we take data security and privacy very seriously. All processing is done with strict privacy controls. While some features may use cloud processing for enhanced capabilities, we don't store your images or videos unless you explicitly choose to save them to your own account.

Do I need an internet connection to use all features?

While an internet connection enhances some advanced features, many core functions of Blind Vision work offline. This ensures reliability in various situations, even when connectivity is limited.

How can I provide feedback about the application?

We welcome your feedback! You can share your thoughts and suggestions through our contact form on this page, or by emailing us directly at dhananjaynarwade1998@gmail.com. Your input helps us improve our services.
