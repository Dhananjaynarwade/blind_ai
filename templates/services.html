How Our Technology Works

Blind Vision utilizes advanced machine learning models and computer vision techniques to process real-time video from your device's camera. Our technology can identify objects, detect obstacles, interpret colors, and understand spatial relationships to provide meaningful information about your surroundings.

All processing is done securely, with privacy as a core principle. While some features may require an internet connection for enhanced capabilities, many core functions work offline for reliability in all situations.
